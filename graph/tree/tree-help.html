Family Tree Browser
  • Sign in to FamilySearch (or add 'sessionId=<sessionId>' to URL)
  • It will start with your person ID in Family Tree. (or add 'pid=<personId>' or 'url=<personUrl>' to URL. Works with "Genealogies" URL, too)
  • Click grey arrows to expand relatives, and light grey ones to hide them.
  • Shift-Cmd/Ctrl-click a person to open them in a new tab in FamilySearch Family Tree.
  • Selecting persons (so expanding relatives works on all of them at once):
    • Click a person to select them.
    • Shift-click to select list of persons in a generation.
    • Command(Mac) or Control(Windows)-click to select/deselect disconnected persons.
  • Keyboard shortcuts:
    • I - Toggle whether to show person IDs.
    • D - Toggle whether to show person Details
    • L - Toggle whether to coLlapse persons.
    • R - Refresh data (after you make a change in Family Tree)
  • Keyboard shortcuts on selected person(s):
    • 1 - Who to make the main ("#1") person in the chart. Center on this person.
    • M - Hide the selected persons ("Me").
    • P, S, C - Show the Parents, Spouses or Children of the selected person(s).
    • Shift-P, S, C - Hide the Parents, Spouses or Children of the selected person(s).
  • Drag background of person chart (or this help box) to move it around, and/or use scrollbars.
